About United Framing Carpenters

    United Framing Carpenters is a dream that became a reality. As a 24 year master carpenter, I always wished there was a way to show our craftsmanship to those that are interested and not in the field. This website unites framing carpenters across the United States and shows their work building custom homes. We produce live footage videos and pictures of job sites across the country. We record from beginning to end on custom homes being built and sell them to the homeowner for a fee. We also upload progress on said house on ther website so that the homeowner can see the latest progress on their house. The end result is a video experience that is sentimental and something you will cherish for years to come.
