Welcome to Our Site

A Few Words About Us


About Us:


"As a 24 year master carpenter, I always wished there was a way to show our craftsmanship to those that are interested and not in the field. United Framing Carpenters is an outlet uniting framing carpenters across the United States showcasing their hardwork building custom homes and additions. You are able to see what exactly goes into building these custom homes and follow along with constant updates to any given project. If this sounds interesting, United Framing Carpenters is for you. I hope you enjoy." - Steven K.



What We Do:


We showcase the hardwork and craftsmanship of carpenters across America through videos and images. You would be able to see the progression of custom homes, house additions, etc. as they are being built. We act as a community host for all content. You (the carpenter or homeowner) can send us images and videos of the work and we will show it to the masses absolutely free.
